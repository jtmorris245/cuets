About Us

The society meets every week (almost without fail) and we hope to always find something you will enjoy building or doing, whatever your previous level of electronics experience. We'll be running a variety of events throughout the year, such as project sessions, talks, trips and (of course) socials. If you have your own ideas then we have the facilities to help you out too!

Just turn up! Our meetings run every Wednesday in E1.13 at 1:30pm. That's the big Electronics Lab in the East Wing of the Engineering Buildings. If you can't find us, just go to the first floor in either the South or West building, and follow it around anti-Clockwise until you see a room labelled E1.13 or ask someone - most people will be able to point you the right way.

If there any questions, don't hesitate to contact commitee members, or join the society group on facebook, and leave a message there. You can even collar us in the corridor. Regardless of that, we look forward to hearing from you soon!
